Dozenal scientific calculator

A full, shell-style scientific calculator using the dozenal numeral system. A and B (both cases allowed) represent 10 and 11.




    Supported functions

    • a + b - addition
    • a - b - subtraction
    • a * b - multiplication
    • a / b - division
    • a % b - modulo/remainder
    • a ^ b - exponential
    • pi() - pi constant
    • e() - e constant
    • sin(a) - sine
    • cos(a) - cosine
    • tan(a) - tangent
    • asin(a) - inverse sine
    • acos(a) - inverse cosine
    • atan(a) - inverse tangent
    • sinh(a) - hyperbolic sine
    • cosh(a) - hyperbolic cosine
    • tanh(a) - hyperbolic tangent
    • asinh(a) - inverse hyperbolic sine
    • acosh(a) - inverse hyperbolic cosine
    • atanh(a) - inverse hyperbolic tangent
    • log(a) - base-e logarithm
    • logz(a) - base-12 logarithm
    • exp(a) - base-e exponential
    • expz(a) - base-12 exponential
    • sqrt(a) - square root
    • gamma(a) - gamma function (Lanczos approximation)
