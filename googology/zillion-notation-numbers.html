Extended Bowers' -illions (Zillion Notation Numbers)

This is a massive extension of Jonathan Bower's -illion system to go far beyond Tier 4. Numbers beyond multillion are all by me. I have defined the -illion numbers with my own -illion shortening notation called Zillion Notation, in which z(x, y) = xth Tier y -illion, because it's too long to write high-tier illions in 10^3x+3 format. Zillion Notation also supports having 3 or more entries and has a similar strength to linear BEAF. Aarex Tiaokhiao and Soulility have helped for some parts of this list.

This is an update of my old "Extended Tier 4-6 Illions". Original Bowers' -illions are also included here for reference.
