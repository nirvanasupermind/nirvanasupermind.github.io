Ampersand Notation

Ampersand Notation (&N) is a notation that can express large numbers. The notation is currently composed of 1 part, which is as follows:

  • Basic Ampersand Notation (B&N)

I plan to create the following parts in the future:

  • Extended Ampersand Notation (X&N)
  • Nested Ampersand Notation (N&N)
