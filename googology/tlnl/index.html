The Longest Number List

This list aims to be the longest and most comprehensive lists of numbers in ascending order, ranging from negative numbers to large transfinite ordinals. It is perpetually under construction. This list contains numbers outsourced from many, many other lists, including but not limited to, Googology Wiki's list of googolisms, Cloudy176's faketest, ARsygo's truetest, and Numberpedia's list of numbers, in addition to many original coinages by me. My own coinages are marked in italics.

This list will include:

  • Well-defined salad numbers/naive extensions (eg. Rayoplex)
  • Transfinite ordinals
  • Complex numbers (sorted by lexicographical order)
  • Numbers whose well-definedness is currently uncertain (eg. Laver table numbers, Large Number Garden Number)

This list will not include:

  • Ill-defined numbers (eg. higher-order BEAF)

This list is split into multiple parts for ease of navigation.

  • Part 1 (<-10100)
  • Part 2 (-10100 - 0)
  • Part 3 (0 - 10)
  • Part 4 (10 - 1000)
  • Part 5 (1000 - 10,000,000,000)
