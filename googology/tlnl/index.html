The Longest Number List

This list aims to be (one of) the longest and most comprehensive lists of numbers in ascending order, ranging from negative numbers to large transfinite ordinals. This list contains numbers outsourced from many, many other lists such as Googology Wiki's list of googolisms, Cloudy176's faketest, and Numberpedia's list of numbers. Don't attempt to use this as a source for Googology Wiki pages because only some of the numbers are actually mine.

This list will include:

  • Original coinages
  • Well-defined salad numbers/naive extensions
  • Transfinite ordinals
  • Complex numbers (sorted by lexicographical order)

This list will not include:

  • Ill-defined numbers (except for higher-order BEAF)

This list is split into multiple parts for ease of navigation.
