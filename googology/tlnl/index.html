The Longest Number List

This list aims to be the longest and most comprehensive lists of numbers in ascending order, ranging from negative numbers to large transfinite ordinals. It is perpetually under construction. In addition to my own original coinages, this list contains numbers outsourced from many, many other lists such as Googology Wiki's list of googolisms and Cloudy176's faketest.

This list will include:

  • Well-defined salad numbers/naive extensions (eg. Rayoplex)
  • Numbers whose well-definedness is currently uncertain (eg. Laver table numbers, Large Number Garden Number)

This list will not include:

  • Ill-defined numbers (eg. higher-order BEAF)

This list is split into multiple parts for ease of navigation.

  • Part 1 (<-{10,100(1)2})
  • Part 2 (-{10,100(1)2} - 0)
  • Part 3 (0 - 1)
  • Part 4 (1 - 10)
  • Part 5 (10 - 1000)
