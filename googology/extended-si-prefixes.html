Extended SI Prefixes

This is an extended SI prefix system that can work for every power of 10 up to 10900. It works by assigning the 10 unused letters in SI prefix symbols (counting u as a substitute for μ), "bhijlosvwx", to random powers of 1030, and usuing compound prefixes to deal with everything else.
