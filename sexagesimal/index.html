The Sexagesimal System

  • Sexagesimal is a numeral system structured around powers of 60 rather than powers of 10. Pure sexagesimal requires the memorization of 60 unique digits, ranging from 0-59, and a 60x60 multiplication table. However, this can be easily avoided by notating each sexagesimal digit as two decimal digits. The number 60 has 12 divisors, so multiplication and division are often simpler in sexagesimal.
